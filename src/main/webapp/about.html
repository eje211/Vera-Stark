Herb Forrester is a filmmaker, musician, and entrepreneur from Oakland, California. He first saw The Belle of New Orleans at the age of 8 and has been a devotee in the cult of Vera Stark ever since. His essays have appeared in a variety of publications, including his own self published, unauthorized biography of Vera Stark.

He is always looking for users to add to Vera's story. It can be as simple as an opinion, a review. Or, include something more involved, like an interpretation or photo artifact.
Herb maintains meetverastark.com.
