A poster for The Belle of New Orleans (1933).
Featuring Gloria Mitchell.
 
This film marked Vera's first appearance on the silver screen. She played the role of Tilly the maid, opposite Gloria Mitchell. Her famous line in the final scene of the movie set the tone for her future career: "Hold on, and together we'll face a new day!"
